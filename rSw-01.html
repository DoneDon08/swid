SW01 Heater

Why SW01?

The SW01 electric heater is a safer, more affordable alternative to gas heaters. We designed it to be lightweight for heavy backpacks, durable for the fields, and compact for easy packing. Unlike gas heaters, it won’t leak and works at any altitude, making it reliable even in extreme conditions. Developed in the Alps, it’s built for adventure.

Timeline

Goals

From the start, our goal was to create a better alternative over portable gas heaters, one that’s more affordable, lighter, and safer for hikers. Additionally, it is rechargeable anywhere using solar panels at no cost, a feature gas heaters lack. It won’t leak, ensuring hiker safety. We also designed it with sustainability in mind. We plan to release two models: basic and premium.
