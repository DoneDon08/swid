About Us

Who are we?

Founded on creativity and problem-solving, Swidor is driven by a passion for innovation. Our mission is to develop smarter solutions that improve everyday life.

Our short story

Our journey began three years ago when I received second place in the Országos Tudományos és Innovációs Verseny (Hungarian Science and Innovation Contest) with a radio-communication-based hit machine for épée fencing. This competition quickly became a core point in my life, with my projects consistently ranking among the top 28.
In 2024, during a live demonstration of my heater project, I realized its potentail due to the feedbacks I received there. That moment marked the turning point—what started as a passion for innovation is now evolving into a real business. With the upcoming launch of our Kickstarter campaign, our official journey is just beginning.
